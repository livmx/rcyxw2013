Plugins in action

Here are the available plugins in action:

  1. autoplay
  2. mousewheel

Autoplay plugin:


First Second Third

Tab Panel 1 Open third tab

Tab Panel 2

Tab Panel 3 Open second tab

jQuery Code:

	$(function(){
		window.api = $('#tabs1').switchable('#panels1 > p', {
			circular: true
		}).autoplay({
			autopause: false,
			api: true
		});
	});

Mousewheel plugin:


First Second Third

Tab Panel 1 Open third tab

Tab Panel 2

Tab Panel 3 Open second tab

jQuery Code:

	$(function(){
		$('#tabs2').switchable('#panels2 > p').mousewheel();
	});
